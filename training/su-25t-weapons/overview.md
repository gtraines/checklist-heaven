## Course Description

The Su-25T Weapons Employment Course (WEC) qualifies a pilot to safely and effectively employ the full range of guided and unguided weapons available on the Su-25T "Frogfoot-B" against ground targets in day VMC conditions.

Upon successful completion, the student will be qualified to:
- Employ the GSh-30-2 gun and unguided rockets (S-8, S-13, S-25) in CCIP from a diving attack profile
- Deliver FAB-series gravity bombs in both CCIP (dive) and CCRP (level) modes
- Employ the 9A4172 Vikhr laser-beam-riding missile using Shkval targeting
- Deliver Kh-25ML and Kh-29L laser-guided missiles
- Conduct SEAD with the Fantasmagoria pod, Kh-58, and Kh-25MPU

Defensive counter-air (DCA), air-to-air employment, and multi-ship coordination are **not** covered in this course.

## Grading Standards

| Grade | Definition |
|---|---|
| **PASS** | All standards met; student may progress |
| **REPEAT** | One or more standards not met; event must be re-flown |
| **DOWN** | Unsafe act — fratricide, uncontrolled flight into terrain, or weapons release outside safe parameters |

### Incomplete Item (II)
An II is issued when a student fails one weapons category but passes others. The student must re-demonstrate the failed weapon type before proceeding to the Check Ride.
