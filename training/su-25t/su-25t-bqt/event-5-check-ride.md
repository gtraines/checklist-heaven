## Check Ride Overview

The Check Ride is a **pass/fail evaluation** of the full Basic Qualification Training syllabus. The student will fly an unassisted, complete profile from cold start to full stop without prompting from an instructor.

The check ride examines everything covered in Events 1–4. No new maneuvers or procedures are introduced.

> **This is not a training event.** The student flies the profile independently. The instructor evaluates but does not coach.

---

## Prerequisites

Before beginning the Check Ride, the student must:

- [ ] Have completed Events 1–4 with no unresolved Incomplete Items (IIs)
- [ ] Be able to state all critical limitations from memory (see Event 1)
- [ ] Have performed a minimum of **three full-stop landings** in Events 2–4 without a tire blowout
- [ ] Not have received a DOWN grade in any prior event

If any prerequisite is unmet, the Check Ride is deferred. Re-fly the deficient event.

---

## Grading Criteria

### Grades
| Grade | Definition |
|---|---|
| **PASS** | All standards met; student is Basic Qualified on the Su-25T |
| **INCOMPLETE (II)** | One or two standards not met, but no unsafe acts; student must re-fly deficient portions only |
| **FAIL** | Three or more standards not met, OR any unsafe act committed |
| **DOWN** | Unsafe act resulting in simulated aircraft loss or threat to operations |

### Unsafe Acts (Automatic DOWN)
The following automatically result in a DOWN grade:
- Takeoff above maximum gross weight
- Stall departure/spin below 3,000 m AGL during airwork
- Runway excursion on takeoff or landing
- Landing with gear retracted
- Blown tire due to braking above 200 km/h

---

## Check Ride Profile

The student will fly the following profile, sequentially, without interruption:

```
1. Cold Start & Engine Run-Up
2. Taxi to Runway
3. Normal Takeoff
4. Departure Climb to 5,000 m MSL
5. ── Airwork Area ──────────────────────────────────────
   a. 30° and 45° banked level turns (both directions)
   b. Wings-level approach-to-stall and recovery
   c. Trim sweep: 350 km/h → 550 km/h → 350 km/h
   d. ACS Emergency Level (LAlt+3) demonstration
6. ── Return ────────────────────────────────────────────
7. Traffic Pattern — first approach
   a. Instructor calls "Go-Around" at 100 m AGL (student must execute correctly)
8. Traffic Pattern — second approach (full stop)
9. Post-landing rollout with drag chute and progressive braking
10. Taxi clear of runway
```

## Post-Check Ride Debrief

### If PASS

Congratulations — the student is **Basic Qualified on the Su-25T Frogfoot-B**.

Recommended follow-on training:
- [Basic & Intermediate Surface Attack Course](../surface-attack-course.md) — weapons employment
- [Formation Flying Training Course](../formation-flying-training.md) — multi-ship operations

### If FAIL or DOWN

1. Debrief all failed items in detail — identify root cause (knowledge gap, technique, or habit)
2. Return to the event that covers the failed area and re-fly it as a training event
3. Schedule a re-check ride after minimum one training event
