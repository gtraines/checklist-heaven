## Course Description

The Su-25T Basic Qualification Training (BQT) course qualifies a pilot to safely operate the Sukhoi Su-25T "Frogfoot-B" in day VMC conditions from cold-and-dark startup through safe aircraft recovery. Combat employment is **not** covered in this course.

Upon successful completion, the student will be qualified to:
- Perform independent cold-start and taxi operations
- Execute a safe takeoff under normal and maximum-effort conditions
- Conduct basic airwork including coordinated turns, climbs, descents, and stall recognition
- Return to and land at a prepared runway using visual traffic pattern procedures
- Deploy the drag chute and execute correct braking technique

## Syllabus Events

Complete events in sequence. Each event must be passed before progressing.

| Event | Title | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Event 1](event-1-academics.md) | Aircraft Systems & Academics | Ground | Systems knowledge, limitations, emergency procedures |
| [Event 2](event-2-fam-1.md) | Familiarization Flight 1 | Flight | Cold start, taxi, takeoff, basic airwork, landing |
| [Event 3](event-3-fam-2.md) | Familiarization Flight 2 | Flight | Stalls, spin avoidance, trim technique, ACS modes |
| [Event 4](event-4-traffic-pattern.md) | Traffic Pattern | Flight | Multiple approaches and landings, drag chute, braking |
| [Event 5](event-5-check-ride.md) | Check Ride | Evaluation | Full-profile evaluation flight — pass/fail |

**Estimated Completion:** 4–6 simulator sessions.

## Grading Standards

### Grades
| Grade | Definition |
|---|---|
| **PASS** | All standards met; student may progress |
| **REPEAT** | One or more standards not met; event must be re-flown |
| **DOWN** | Unsafe act observed; event grounded pending debrief |

### Incomplete Item (II)
An II is issued when a student fails to meet a specific standard but passes the overall event. An II must be resolved before the Check Ride (Event 5).
