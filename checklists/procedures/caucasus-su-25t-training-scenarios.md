## Scenario 1 — IRON ANVIL 01-T (Phase 1: Basic)

> **Focus:** Unguided bombs/rockets/gun, box pattern discipline, safe escape

### Location: Kobuleti North Training Area — "The Polygon"

- **IP/Run-in:** Red smoke on abandoned strip south of target; run-in heading 360°. Pattern altitude 1,500 ft MSL (≈1,470 AGL). Right-hand box 1.5 NM east of strip over marshland.
- **Target Set:** Truck convoy (Ural-375 ×4) on abandoned strip; secondary strafe pit adjacent.

### Loadout (Su-25T)
- 2× FAB-250, 2× B-8M (S-8 rockets), full GSh-30-2, 96 flares (no chaff). Optional SPS-141 if mission allows.

### Execution
1. Depart **Kobuleti RWY 07**, climb to 1,500 ft MSL, track KBL 360 radial to 5 DME.
2. Enter right-hand box; fly three dry laps before first hot pass.
3. **Bomb pass:** 20–25° dive from 11–12k ft MSL; release ≥4.5k ft AGL; "IN" / "OFF".
4. **Rocket pass:** 10–15° dive from 3.5–4.5k ft MSL; slant 1.5–2.5 km; "IN" / "OFF".
5. **Gun pass:** 10–15°; cease fire ≥1.5–2.0k ft AGL; "GUNS GUNS GUNS".
6. Egress east over water; RTB Kobuleti with fuel above Bingo.

### Threat / Constraints
- No air defences. Enforce frag floors; avoid airbrake + high-G pulls low.

### Success Criteria
- 1 bomb within 25 m CEP, rockets/gun observed on target, correct brevity, no floor busts.

## Scenario 2 — PRECISION STRIKE 02-T (Phase 2: Precision and Systems)

> **Focus:** Shkval/laser work, guided stores, laser discipline

### Location: Vaziani Range (east of base)

- **IP/Run-in:** Orange panel/smoke at IP on 100° radial, 6 DME from VAZ. Employment block 6–10k ft MSL for Vikhr; 10–12k ft for Kh-25/29/KAB.
- **Target Set:** T-1 Bunker (static, hardened), T-2 Armour (T-72 ×3, weapons hold), T-3 Soft (Ural convoy ×4).

### Loadout (Su-25T)
- 2× twin Vikhr racks (4 missiles), 2× Kh-25ML **or** 2× Kh-29T, 1× Mercury pod optional (night), full gun, flares max.

### Execution
1. Depart **Vaziani RWY 13**, turn 100°, climb to block. Cage/uncage Shkval; confirm laser timer.
2. Acquire T-2 Armour with wide→narrow FOV; **Vikhr** pairs at 6–8 km, wings-level; lase through TOF, reset timer.
3. Attack T-1 Bunker with **Kh-25ML** (3–8 km, shallow 5–10°) or **Kh-29T** (TV lock 6–10 km, level).
4. If laser overtemp or lock lost, call **"CONTINUE DRY"**, cool 30–40 s, reattack.
5. Egress west; RTB Vaziani.

### Threat / Constraints
- Light MANPADS near T-2; remain ≥6k ft AGL during holds, beam/terrain mask on spike. Laser duty cycle enforced.

### Success Criteria
- ≥2 guided hits (Vikhr + Kh-series) within target areas; no laser overheat aborts; stable locks maintained.

## Scenario 3 — CLOSE FIGHT 03-T (Phase 3: CAS JTAC)

> **Focus:** 9-Line execution, talk-on with Shkval, keyhole geometry, mixed ordnance

### Location: Gori Valley — Urban Fringe

- **IP/Keyhole:** IP WEST (N 41°58'00\" E 44°03'00\") holding west of city at 10–12k ft MSL. Keyhole: Centered on Gori TV tower; use cardinal legs at 8–10 NM.
- **JTAC:** Simulated ground JTAC on 30.000 FM; laser **OFF** by default (forces own marking).
- **Target Set:** 
  - T-1: Technicals (ZU-23 on trucks) in warehouse lot.
  - T-2: Infantry squad in tree line.
  - T-3: Parked APC (BMP-2) near rail yard spur.

### Loadout (Su-25T)
- Mixed: 2× B-8M (S-8), 2× Vikhr twin racks (4 missiles), 1× Kh-25ML (optional), smoke rockets optional, full flares, gun.

### Execution
1. Check-in with JTAC; receive 9-Line; read back. Hold west (10–12k ft MSL) outside MANPADS WEZ.
2. Talk-on using Shkval narrow; confirm **CONTACT** / **TALLY** before clearance.
3. First mark with smoke rocket if JTAC requests; otherwise employ **Vikhr** on T-3, **S-8** on T-1/T-2 area.
4. Maintain laser discipline; if clearance delayed, remain masked and cool laser.
5. Report **"IN"** with attack axis, **"OFF"** with egress direction. Egress south along river to mask.

### Threat / Constraints
- MANPADS / ZU-23 short-range. Stay ≥8k ft AGL in holds; pop only for mark/engagement. No chaff—beam threats.

### Success Criteria
- Correct 9-Line flow and readbacks; at least one guided and one unguided successful attack; no friendly/urban collateral (stay inside target boxes).

## Scenario 4 — IRON CONTROL 04-T (Phase 4: FAC-A)

> **Focus:** Stack management with limited sensors, marking, single-type control

### Location: High Passes North of Sukhumi

- **Stack:** Anchor over ridge line 3–5 NM north of UGSS at 10–15k ft MSL. Offset 3–5 NM east/west for terrain masking.
- **Shooter Flight:** Additional Su-25T element simulated/AI. Frag/order of events controlled by FAC-A.
- **Target Set:** Mixed column (2× BMP-2, 4× trucks) on mountain road; secondary ammo dump (static) on ridgeline shelf.

### Loadout (Su-25T)
- Marking: 2× B-8M (S-8 smoke if available) or standard S-8. Self-escort: 2× Vikhr twin racks (4 missiles) or 1× Kh-25ML pair. Full flares, light fuel.

### Execution
1. Establish stack; brief jink direction and threat lanes (SAM rings marked on F10). Maintain SPO-15 scan.
2. Mark target with smoke rockets; call **"MARK ON DECK"** / **"CONTACT"**. Provide talk-on with bearing/range/landmark from Shkval view.
3. Clear shooter: **"CLEARED HOT"** or **"CONTINUE DRY"**; monitor laser dwell times.
4. If threat pops (Strela/OSA), beam/terrain mask; re-establish stack at offset.
5. BHA collection; re-attack only if safe and fuel allows.

### Threat / Constraints
- Light SHORAD (Strela/OSA) in valley; avoid loitering inside WEZ. No chaff—use terrain and offsets. Night optional with Mercury (limit to <4–5G).

### Success Criteria
- Accurate mark and talk-on; shooter deliveries within target area; no threat-driven losses; disciplined stack altitude deconfliction.
